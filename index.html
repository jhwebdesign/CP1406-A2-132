Townsville's Very
Own Orchestra

Fast becoming one of North Queensland's cultural jewels, the Barrier Reef Orchestra this year launches its 17th year of performing orchestral music through North Queensland. BRO is a community orchestra consisting of the finest musicians drawn from Townsville and as far afield as Cairns and Mackay.
